## 2. Onboarding Flow (PLG)

Step 1: Land on published dashboard or content (SEO) → Step 2: Sign up (email or SSO) → Step 3: Create workspace → Step 4: Guided first task ("Analyze a buyer" or "See your market dashboard") → Step 5: See value (credit analysis result or market insight) → Step 6: Explore features (channels, agents, policies) → Step 7: Invite team members → Step 8: Convert to paid tier

**Target: Time to first value < 5 minutes.**

## 3. SEO Strategy (from Arquitetura Agêntica Report)

Publish the 70+ territory research reports as public, indexable content. Each report:

- Targets specific long-tail keywords (e.g., "liquidez CRI mercado imobiliário")
- Links to the Liquid platform for deeper analysis
- Creates authority in the domain
- Drives organic traffic from stakeholders researching market topics

## 4. Community Strategy (Camada 0)

**Community before product.** Build the community of RE credit stakeholders first:

- Knowledge base as public good (70+ reports)
- Published dashboards as shared market intelligence
- Future: events, webinars, meetups for RE credit professionals
- Founding member program (50+ incorporadoras target in first 90 days)
