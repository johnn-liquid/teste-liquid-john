## 5. Criteria for Advancing to the Next Opportunity

| Transition | Gate Criteria |
| --- | --- |
| **Phase 1 → Phase 2** | At least 5 incorporadoras actively using agentic repasse; Liquid Pass v2 in production; SaaS MRR covers operational costs |
| **Phase 2 → Phase 3** | Market intelligence dashboard driving organic traffic; portfolio assessment used by 3+ institutions; monitoring subscription active |
| **Phase 3 → Phase 4** | First liquidity transactions with take rate closed; marketplace has both sellers and buyers; unit economics validated |
