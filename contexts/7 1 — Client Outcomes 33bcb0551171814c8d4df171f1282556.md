## Outcomes by Persona

### Incorporadora

| Outcome | Primary Metric | Baseline (Today) | Target (12mo) | How to Measure | Leading Indicator |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Faster repasse** | Days from contract to bank transfer | 60-120 days | 30-45 days | Median days in repasse pipeline | # of blockers identified per contract |
| **Lower default rate** | % of approved buyers that default | Market average 5-8% | < 3% | Default tracking post-approval | Risk score accuracy at approval |
| **Reduced operational cost** | Cost per repasse operation | ~R$500-1000/contract (manual) | < R$200/contract (agent-assisted) | Total cost / completed contracts | Agent task completion rate |
| **Better portfolio visibility** | Time to generate portfolio report | Days (manual) | Minutes (agent) | Report generation time | Dashboard usage frequency |
| **Liquidity access** | Time to transaction | Months | Weeks | Days from scoring to transaction | Kernel score improving trend |

### Bank

| Outcome | Primary Metric | Baseline | Target | Leading Indicator |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| **Faster processing** | Days to process credit application | 5-15 days | 1-3 days | Pre-analysis completion at submission |
| **Better compliance** | Audit findings per period | Variable | Zero critical | Activity log completeness |

### Securitizadora / Fund

| Outcome | Primary Metric | Baseline | Target | Leading Indicator |
