## 1. Role of the Product in the Company

**Product as Core — everything orbits it.**

The product is not a department’s output. It IS the company. The brain dumps are explicit: "A companhia humana deve ser criada no entorno da máquina, e não o contrário." The human organization exists to build, feed, and scale the platform. Every role (engineering, context engineering, operations, sales) is defined by its relationship to the product.

Implications:

- Product metrics = company metrics
- Product growth = company growth (PLG)
- Product quality = brand quality
- Product architecture = company architecture

---

## 2. Role of the Product in the Market

**Liquid OS is the operating system for real estate credit liquidity.**

It replaces:

- Fragmented bureau queries → with unified, agent-powered credit intelligence
- Manual analyst teams → with agent-assisted operations
- Siloed CRM/spreadsheet workflows → with governed multi-stakeholder channels
- Opaque credit decisions → with auditable policy-as-code
- Manual liquidity operations → with trigger-driven, agent-facilitated transactions
- Expensive consulting engagements → with always-on market intelligence

It creates:

- A proprietary knowledge base about the real estate credit market that improves over time
- Network effects across multiple stakeholder types
- A governed path from intelligence to liquidity

## 4. Organizing Principle of the Product

**The product is organized around the liquidity funnel:**

Wide entry (intelligence, community) → Deep engagement (operations, agents, policies) → Narrow exit (liquidity transactions, take rate)

Every entity, every feature, every agent serves this funnel. If something doesn’t contribute to moving stakeholders along this funnel, it needs justification (Product Constitution C1).
