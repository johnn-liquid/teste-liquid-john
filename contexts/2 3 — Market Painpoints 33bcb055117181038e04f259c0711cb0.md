# 2.3 — Market Painpoints

> **Layer:** 2 — Problem & Opportunity Framing
> 

> **Question this answers:** What are the validated and assumed pains, ranked by actor and intensity?
> 

> **Last updated:** April 2026
> 

> **Sources:** CEO Braindump, Soma Repasse operations, Liquid Pass client feedback, Brain Dumps, Territory Research
> 

---

## 1. Validated Pains (Evidence Exists)

| # | Pain | Segment | Validation Source | Intensity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| VP1 | **Repasse takes too long** — average 60-120 days from contract to bank transfer completion | Incorporadoras | Soma operations (~962 contracts) — directly observed | 🔴 Critical |
| VP2 | **Credit decisions are inconsistent** — same buyer gets different outcomes from different analysts/processes | Incorporadoras, Banks | Liquid Pass v1 client adoption confirms pain exists | 🔴 Critical |
| VP3 | **Manual bureau queries are expensive and slow** — each credit check requires manual orchestration of 3-5 data sources | Incorporadoras | Liquid Pass feature adoption (multi-bureau integration is most valued) | 🟠 High |
| VP4 | **No audit trail for credit decisions** — compliance risk increases with regulatory scrutiny | Banks, Incorporadoras | Client requests for compliance features in Liquid Pass | 🟠 High |
| VP5 | **Portfolio data quality blocks transactions** — sellers can’t efficiently package portfolios for sale | Securitizadoras, Portfolio holders | Transação Carteira operations confirm | 🟠 High |
| VP6 | **Repasse operations require dedicated human teams** — no scalable alternative exists | Incorporadoras | Soma’s own scaling challenge validates this | 🟠 High |
| VP7 | **CA-600 certification is a manual bottleneck** | Incorporadoras, Banks | Liquid Pass feature for CA-600 automation addresses this | 🟡 Medium |
